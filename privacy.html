Reddit Feed Cleaner

Chrome Extension — Privacy Policy
Effective date: June 29, 2026

Short version: Reddit Feed Cleaner collects no personal data, transmits nothing off your device, and requires no account. Your filter preferences are stored locally on your machine only. That's it.

1. What This Extension Does

Reddit Feed Cleaner is a Chrome extension that filters Reddit feed posts by type (Text, Image, Video, Link) in real time. It reads the structure of Reddit's page to classify and hide or show posts based on your preferences.

It does not read post content for any purpose other than matching your locally-stored keyword blocklist — and even that processing happens entirely in memory on your device and is never stored or transmitted anywhere.

3. What Is Stored Locally

The extension stores the following data in chrome.storage.local — this is sandboxed storage on your own device, accessible only to this extension:

  • Your filter toggle preferences (which post types to show or hide)
  • Your keyword blocklist (up to 5 keywords in the free tier)
  • Your active preset selection

This data never leaves your device. It is never synced, transmitted, or shared with anyone — including us.

Stored locally only Never transmitted No account required

4. Permissions Explained

  • storage — Used to save your filter preferences locally on your device.
  • activeTab — Used to send filter updates to the current Reddit tab when you change settings. No tab data is read or stored.
  • Host permission (reddit.com) — Required to inject the content script that classifies and filters posts on Reddit's feed pages.

5. Third Parties

Reddit Feed Cleaner does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No third-party SDKs are included in the extension.

This extension is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or in any way officially connected with Reddit, Inc.

6. Remote Code

This extension does not execute any remote code. All logic is bundled locally within the extension package. No eval(), no dynamic script loading, and no external scripts are injected at runtime.

7. Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, this extension is safe for use by anyone.
